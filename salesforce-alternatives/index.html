Best Salesforce alternatives for 2026

Independent picks. Affiliate links noted.

When your sales team spends more time managing Salesforce than selling, or you need a CRM that works out of the box without a dedicated admin, it may be time to explore simpler, more focused alternatives. The right fit depends on your team’s size, sales cycle, and whether you need tightly integrated marketing or a pipeline that’s dead simple to manage.

The best alternatives to Salesforce

  • HubSpot CRM
    HubSpot CRM is widely used for its unified platform that brings marketing, sales, and service together without heavy configuration. It’s a strong choice for growing teams that want to expand into a full suite without the overhead of a more complex system.
    Visit site: HubSpot CRM →
  • Pipedrive
    Pipedrive is known for its clean, visual pipeline that puts your deals front and center with minimal setup. It’s frequently recommended for small and mid-size sales teams that value clarity over heavy customization.
    Visit site: Pipedrive →
  • Zoho CRM
    Zoho CRM offers deep customization and a broad feature set at a price point that’s accessible for cost-conscious teams. It’s especially effective when paired with other Zoho business apps, creating a seamless ecosystem.
    Visit site: Zoho CRM →
  • Close
    Close is purpose-built for inside sales and SDR teams that rely on high call volume and automated outreach sequences. Users often highlight its integrated calling and streamlined workflow as a major productivity boost.
    Visit site: Close →
  • monday CRM
    Monday CRM slots directly into the monday.com workspace, making it perfect for teams already using that platform. It also stands out for its flexibility with non-linear or project-based sales processes that don’t fit a traditional pipeline.
    Visit site: monday CRM →
